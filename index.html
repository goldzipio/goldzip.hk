GOLDZIP represents the perfect alternative to a direct purchase of gold bullion.

THE CHINESE GOLD & SILVER EXCHANGE SOCIETY (CGSE)

Founded in 1910, the Chinese Gold and Silver Exchange Society (“CGSE”) is the sole exchange in Hong Kong which trades physical gold and silver. Since inception, this home-grown, member-owned and self-regulated exchange has developed and expanded to become a thriving metal exchange with a solid foundation and strong tradition.

CGSE is a metal exchange which combines both spot and futures contracts. In addition to delivery of physical metals, the trading parties can offer to pay a premium to defer the contract delivery, thereby performing the function of futures market. This unique trading mechanism was the brainchild of the early founders of CGSE, who through ingenuity and foresight created a platform that is still in use today, standing the test of time, and operating today as smoothly and effectively as in the early years.

中国金银贸易场（CGSE）

中国金银业贸易场（CGSE）创立于1910年，是香港唯一一家实体金银交易所。创立至今，这家扎根本土、成员共同拥有、自我约束的交易所不断发展扩张。经过数年的蓬勃发展，CGSE成为香港，乃至全球范围内根基稳健、欣欣向荣的金属交易所。

CGSE同时处理现货和期货。除了实体黄金的交付，交易方还可以预付定金，延期合同交付，从而实现期货市场的功能。这个独有机制是CGSE的创始人多年前集思广益，花费大量人力物力创造出来的成果。该交易机制经受住了时间的考验，可以高效流畅地运行。
